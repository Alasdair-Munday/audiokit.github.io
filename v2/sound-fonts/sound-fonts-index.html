--- title: AudioKit Sound Fonts header: Sound Fonts permalink: /v2/sound-fonts/ layout: v2_section_index --- As of AudioKit 2.2 there is support for Sound Fonts and an included General MIDI sound font.

Sound Fonts

ClassDescription
AKSoundFont A reference lookup number for the sound font.
AKSoundFontInstrument Unique number assigned to the instrument by the sound font
AKSoundFontInstrumentPlayer Plays a SoundFont2 (SF2) sample instrument, generating a stereo sound with cubic interpolation.
AKSoundFontPreset The AudioKit class for sound font presets
AKSoundFontPresetPlayer Plays a SoundFont2 (SF2) sample preset, generating a stereo sound with cubic interpolation.
