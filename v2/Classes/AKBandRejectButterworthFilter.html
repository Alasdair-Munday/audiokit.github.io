AKBandRejectButterworthFilter.m

Overview

A band-reject Butterworth filter. These filters are Butterworth second-order IIR filters. They offer an almost flat passband and very good precision and stopband attenuation.

Properties

Bandwidth of the band-reject filters. [Default Value: 2000]
