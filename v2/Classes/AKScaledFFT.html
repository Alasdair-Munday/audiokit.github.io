Overview

Scale the frequency components of a pv stream, resulting in pitch shift. Output amplitudes can be optionally modified in order to attempt formant preservation. The quality of the pitch shift will be improved with the use of a Hann window in the pvoc analysis. Liftered Cepstrum Formant preservation method is less intensive than the true envelope method, which might not be suited to realtime use.
