AKReverb.m

Overview

8 delay line stereo FDN reverb 8 delay line stereo FDN reverb, with feedback matrix based upon physical modeling scattering junction of 8 lossless waveguides of equal characteristic impedance.

Properties

cutoffFrequency

Low-pass cutoff frequency [Default Value: 4000]
{% highlight objective-c %}@property (nonatomic) AKParameter *cutoffFrequency{% endhighlight %}

feedback

Feedback level in the range 0 to 1. 0.6 gives a good small 'live' room sound, 0.8 a small hall, and 0.9 a large hall. A setting of exactly 1 means infinite length, while higher values will make the opcode unstable. [Default Value: 0.6]
{% highlight objective-c %}@property (nonatomic) AKParameter *feedback{% endhighlight %}
