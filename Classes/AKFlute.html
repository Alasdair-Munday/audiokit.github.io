Inherits from AKAudio : AKParameter : NSObject
Declared in AKFlute.h

Overview

A physical model of a flute

Audio output is a tone similar to a flute, using a physical model developed from Perry Cook

initWithFrequency:attackTime:releaseTime:airJetPressure:jetrf:endrf:noiseAmplitude:amplitude:vibratoShape:vibratoAmplitude:vibratoFrequency:

Instantiates the flute with all values

- (instancetype)initWithFrequency:(AKParameter *)frequency attackTime:(AKConstant *)attackTime releaseTime:(AKConstant *)releaseTime airJetPressure:(AKParameter *)airJetPressure jetrf:(AKConstant *)jetrf endrf:(AKConstant *)endrf noiseAmplitude:(AKParameter *)noiseAmplitude amplitude:(AKParameter *)amplitude vibratoShape:(AKFunctionTable *)vibratoShape vibratoAmplitude:(AKParameter *)vibratoAmplitude vibratoFrequency:(AKParameter *)vibratoFrequency

Parameters

frequency

Frequency of the note. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 440]

attackTime

Time in seconds to reach full blowing pressure. [Default Value: 0.1]

releaseTime

Time in seconds taken to stop blowing. [Default Value: 0.1]

airJetPressure

a parameter controlling the air jet. Values should be positive, and the useful range is approximately 0.08 to 0.56. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.2]

jetrf

Amount of reflection in the breath jet that powers the flute. [Default Value: 0.5]

endrf

Reflection coefficient of the breath jet. [Default Value: 0.5]

noiseAmplitude

Amplitude of the noise component, about 0 to 0.5 Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.15]

amplitude

Amplitude of the note, up to but not including 1. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.5]

vibratoShape

Function table defining the shape of the vibrato. [Default Value: sine]

vibratoAmplitude

Amplitude of the vibrato. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0]

vibratoFrequency

Frequency of vibrato in Hertz. Suggested range is 0 to 12 Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0]
