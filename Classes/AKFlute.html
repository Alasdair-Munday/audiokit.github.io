Overview

  • frequency: Frequency of the note. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 440]
  • attackTime: Time in seconds to reach full blowing pressure. [Default Value: 0.1]
  • releaseTime: Time in seconds taken to stop blowing. [Default Value: 0.1]
  • airJetPressure: a parameter controlling the air jet. Values should be positive, and the useful range is approximately 0.08 to 0.56. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.2]
  • jetrf: Amount of reflection in the breath jet that powers the flute. [Default Value: 0.5]
  • endrf: Reflection coefficient of the breath jet. [Default Value: 0.5]
  • noiseAmplitude: Amplitude of the noise component, about 0 to 0.5 Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.15]
  • amplitude: Amplitude of the note, up to but not including 1. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.5]
  • vibratoShape: Table defining the shape of the vibrato. [Default Value: sine]
  • vibratoAmplitude: Amplitude of the vibrato. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0]
  • vibratoFrequency: Frequency of vibrato in Hertz. Suggested range is 0 to 12 Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0]
