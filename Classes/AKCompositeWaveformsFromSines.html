Instantiates a composite waveform of weighted sinusoids from separate arrays describing parital properties. Arrays must be equal in length.

- (instancetype)initWithTableSize:(int)tableSize partialNumbers:(AKArray *)partialNumbers partialStrengths:(AKArray *)partialStrengths partialStrengthOffsets:(AKArray *)partialOffsets partialPhases:(AKArray *)partialPhases

Parameters

tableSize

Number of points in the table. Must be a power of 2 or power-of-2 plus 1.

partialNumbers

Array containing partial numbers (relative to a fundamental that would occupy tableSize locations per cycle) of sinusoid a, sinusoid b, etc. Must contain positive values, but need not be a whole numbers, i.e., non-harmonic partials are permitted. Partials may be in any order.

partialStrengths

Array containing strengths of partials pna, pnb, etc. These are relative strengths, since the composite waveform may be rescaled later. Negative values are permitted and imply a 180 degree phase shift.

partialOffsets

Array of DC offset of partials pna, pnb, etc. This is applied after strength scaling, i.e. a value of 2 will lift a 2-strength sinusoid from range [-2,2] to range [0,4] (before later rescaling).

partialPhases

Array of initial phase of partials pna, pnb, etc., expressed in degrees.
