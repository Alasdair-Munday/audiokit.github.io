Overview

Semi-physical model of a cabasa sound. This one of the PhISEM percussion opcodes. PhISEM (Physically Informed Stochastic Event Modeling) is an algorithmic approach for simulating collisions of multiple independent sound producing objects.

Properties

Amplitude of output. As these instruments are stochastic this is only a approximation. [Default Value: 1]
