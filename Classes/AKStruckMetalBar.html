Overview

Creates a tone similar to a struck metal bar.

Audio output is a tone similar to a struck metal bar, using a physical model developed from solving the partial differential equation. There are controls over the boundary conditions as well as the bar characteristics.

Parameters

decayTime

30 db decay time in seconds.

dimensionlessStiffness

Dimensionless stiffness parameter. If this parameter is negative then the initialization is skipped and the previous state of the bar is continued.

highFrequencyLoss

high-frequency loss parameter (keep this small).

strikePosition

Position along the bar that the strike occurs.

strikeVelocity

Normalized strike velocity.

strikeWidth

Spatial width of strike.

leftBoundaryCondition

Boundary condition at left end of bar (1 is clamped; 2 pivoting and 3 free).

rightBoundaryCondition

Boundary condition at right end of bar (1 is clamped; 2 pivoting and 3 free).

scanSpeed

Speed of scanning the output location.
