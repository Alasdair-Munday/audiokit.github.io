Overview

Creates a tone similar to a struck metal bar.

Audio output is a tone similar to a struck metal bar, using a physical model developed from solving the partial differential equation. There are controls over the boundary conditions as well as the bar characteristics. More information regarding scanned synthesis can be found at http://dafx12.york.ac.uk/papers/dafx12_submission_18.pdf and http://www.billverplank.com/ScannedSynthesis.PDF
