Overview

Basic frequency modulated oscillator with linear interpolation.

Classic FM Synthesis audio generation.

Parameters

functionTable

Function table to use. Requires a wrap-around guard point. [Default Value: sine]

baseFrequency

In cycles per second, or Hz, this is the common denominator for the carrier and modulating frequencies. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 440]

carrierMultiplier

This multiplied by the baseFrequency gives the carrier frequency. [Default Value: 1]

modulatingMultiplier

This multiplied by the baseFrequency gives the modulating frequency. [Default Value: 1]

modulationIndex

This multiplied by the modulating frequency gives the modulation amplitude. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 1]

This multiplied by the modulating frequency gives the modulation amplitude. [Default Value: 0.5]
