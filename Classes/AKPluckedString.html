Overview

Physical model of the plucked string.

A user can control the pluck point, the pickup point, the filter, and an additional audio signal used to excite the ‘string’. Based on the Karplus-Strong algorithm.

Parameters

frequency

Frequency of the string [Default Value: 440]

pluckPosition

The point of pluck as a fraction of the way up the string (0 to 1). A pluck point of zero means no initial pluck. [Default Value: 0.01]

samplePosition

Proportion of the way along the string to sample the output. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.1]

reflectionCoefficient

The coefficient of reflection, indicating the lossiness and the rate of decay. It must be strictly between 0 and 1 (it will complain about both 0 and 1). Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.1]

amplitude

Amplitude of note. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 1.0]
