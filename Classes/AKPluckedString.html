AKPluckedString.m

Overview

Physical model of the plucked string. A user can control the pluck point, the pickup point, the filter, and an additional audio signal used to excite the 'string'. Based on the Karplus-Strong algorithm.

  • frequency: Frequency of the string [Default Value: 440]
  • pluckPosition: The point of pluck as a fraction of the way up the string (0 to 1). A pluck point of zero means no initial pluck. [Default Value: 0.01]
  • samplePosition: Proportion of the way along the string to sample the output. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.1]
