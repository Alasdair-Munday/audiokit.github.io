Overview

Second-order tunable equalisation filter based on Regalia and Mitra design. Provides a peak/notch filter for building parametric/graphic equalisers. The amplitude response for this filter will be flat (=1) for gain=1. With gain bigger than 1, there will be a peak at the center frequency, whose width is given by the bandwidth parameter, but outside this band, the response will tend towards 1. Conversely, if gain is smaller than 1, a notch will be created around the CF.
