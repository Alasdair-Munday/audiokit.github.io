AKBallWithinTheBoxReverb.m

Overview

A physical model reverberator. Physical model reverberator based on the paper by Davide Rocchesso 'The Ball within the Box - a sound-processing metaphor', Computer Music Journal, Vol 19, N.4, pp.45-47, Winter 1995.
