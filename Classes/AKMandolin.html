Overview

An emulation of a mandolin.

A mandolin emulation with amplitude, frequency, tuning, gain and mandolin size parameters.

bodySize

The size of the body of the mandolin. Range 0 (small) to 1 (large). Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.5]

frequency

Frequency of note played. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 220]

Amplitude of note. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 1]

pairedStringDetuning

The proportional detuning between the two strings. Suggested range 0.9 to 1. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 1]

pluckPosition

The pluck position, in range 0 to 1. [Default Value: 0.4]

The loop gain of the model, in the range 0.97 to 1. Updated at Control-rate. [Default Value: 0.99]
