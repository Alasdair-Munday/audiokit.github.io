Overview

Generates indicated wave with amplitude declick ramps of .02 sec on each end. The frequency can be given either as pitch or in Hz, and the type can be a sine, triangle, saw, square, tube distortion, half triangle, half square, half-saw, or white noise.

Available Types:

  • Sine
  • Triangle
  • Saw
  • Square
  • Tube Distortion
  • Half Triangle
  • Half Square
  • Half Saw
  • White Noise (in this case, frequency has no meaning)

pitchOrFrequency

Pitch is assume if the value is less than 20, otherwise the units are Hz.

Maximum output of the signal in relation to the 0dB full scale amplitude. Must be greater than zero.
