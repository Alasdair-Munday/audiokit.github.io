We are a team of audio designers, programmers, and musicians dedicated to the goal of making AudioKit the easiest possible audio platform to adopt. We have founded a company as well to support enterprise applications and professional development.

If you run into any problems, go to GitHub and open a ticket. We'll work to address the issue as soon as we can. If you have any other questions about AudioKit, consulting, training, or sound design, send an email to audiokit@audiokit.io. We're looking forward to hearing from you!
