Aurelius Prochazka
aure.com

I am the primary programmer of Audio Kit and I maintain its code base and this web site. I co-founded the company, Hear For Yourself, with Adam to support enterprise applications using Audio Kit. Adam and I are both available for consulting, training, or sound design, so feel free to contact us.


Adam Boulanger
adamboulanger.com

Adam is co-founder of Hear For Yourself and has a long history in computer audio. As an entrepreneur, he develops new technologies to demonstrate how cognitive data can be mined from the auditory experiences of everyday life and conversely, how audio experience can be driven by data.


Nick Arner
nickarner.com

Nick was one of the early adopters of Audio Kit and has contributed example projects and created many of the tutorials and example pages on this web site. He's also available for sound design, workshops, and audio development contracting.


If you run into any problems, go to GitHub and open a ticket. We'll work to address the issue as soon as we can. If you have any questions about Audio Kit, send an email to "audiokit@hearforyourself.com". We're looking forward to hearing from you!
