--- title: About Audio Kit header: About Us permalink: /about/ layout: about ---

We are a team of audio designers, programmers, and musicians dedicated to the goal of making Audio Kit the easiest possible audio platform to adopt. We have founded a company, Hear For Yourself, to support enterprise applications and professional development.

If you run into any problems, go to GitHub and open a ticket. We'll work to address the issue as soon as we can. If you have any other questions about Audio Kit, consulting, training, or sound design, send an email to audiokit@hearforyourself.com. We're looking forward to hearing from you!
